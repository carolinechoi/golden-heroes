Make someone's day with Golden Heroes.

Want to join in on our mission? Make chemotherapy care kits with your school, club, organization, or friends and donate them to your local children's hospital!

Who Are We?

Golden Heroes is a nonprofit organization bringing comfort and smiles to kids with cancer one chemo kit at a time.
